Our Mission:

Our website serves as the ultimate SpaceX launch dashboard, providing you with the most up-to-date and comprehensive information about all things SpaceX. Our website delivers everything you need to know about SpaceX launches, all in one place. Whether you're a space enthusiast or just curious about SpaceX's latest launches, our mission is to keep you informed and engaged with the latest happenings in the space industry. Join us on our journey to explore the unknown reaches of space with SpaceX.

How It Works

In order to keep you informed and engaged with the latest information on SpaceX launches, and to help you explore the unknown reaches of space. We utilize a variety of API's, including the SpaceX API to gather launch information, the NASA API to display the image of the day, and the p5.js Globe API to create an interactive globe that displays the location of each launch. Join us on our journey and discover the excitement of space exploration with SpaceX.
